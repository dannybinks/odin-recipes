Cold Refreshing Watermelon Delight

Is there anything better on a hot summer day than fresh watermelon and a cold drink? Yes there is! A cold Refreshing watermelon delight drink!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Blend watermelon, ice cubes, and honey in a blender until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!
